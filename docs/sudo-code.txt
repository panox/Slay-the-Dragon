One Hero with Health 100
One Enemy with Health 200

Player Turn
  Two Buttons
  One button Attack
    Generates a random number between 3-10 and subtracts it form the enemy
  One button Heal
    Generates a random number between 4-20 and adds it to hero health

Enemy Turn
  Attack
  Random Number between 5-18
  Subtract Attack Number from Hero Health

End of Turn
Show how much healt was lost or gained for each character

End of Game
When one character's health reaches 0
